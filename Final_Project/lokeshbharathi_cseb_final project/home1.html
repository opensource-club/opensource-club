Welcome to the Open Source Technology Club

At the Open Source Technology Club, we are dedicated to create a community of innovators, developers, and tech enthusiasts who are passionate about open-source software. Join us to learn, collaborate, and contribute to exciting projects that make a difference.

Why Join Us?

  • Learn cutting-edge technologies and tools used in the open-source community.
  • Contribute to real-world projects that enhance your resume and portfolio.
  • Connect with like-minded students and professionals who share your passion for technology.
  • Participate in workshops, hackathons, and coding events.

Upcoming Events

We host a variety of events throughout the year, including coding workshops, guest lectures, and collaborative hackathons. Check out our Events page to stay updated on what’s coming next!

Join Our Community

Whether you’re a seasoned developer or just starting out, the Open Source Technology Club is the perfect place to grow your skills and contribute to something meaningful. Join us today!
