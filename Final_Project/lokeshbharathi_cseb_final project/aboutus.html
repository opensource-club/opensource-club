About Us

The Open Source Technology Club of BS.AbdurRahman crescent institute of science and technology is a organization dedicated to the promotion and development of open-source software. Our mission is to empower students to contribute to the open-source community, learn new skills, and collaborate on meaningful projects that benefit everyone.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of open-source technology to drive innovation and make the world a better place. Our mission is to create a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about open-source software and its potential to solve real-world problems.

What We Do

  • Host workshops and tutorials on open-source tools and technologies.
  • Organize hackathons and coding events to encourage collaboration and creativity.
  • Support students in contributing to open-source projects and building their portfolios.
  • Collaborate with other clubs and organizations to promote open-source culture on campus.
  • Organize bootcamp sessions on particular topics for students to gain knowledge about it.
