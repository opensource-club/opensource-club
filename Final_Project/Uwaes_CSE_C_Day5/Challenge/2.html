    Open Source Club

    We present to you the all new Open Source Club of the Department of CSE at B S Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science & Technology., a vibrant community of students and enthusiasts dedicated to the exploration, development, and promotion of open-source technologies. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills to contribute to the global open-source movement, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning. Through workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects, we provide a platform for members to engage with the latest in open-source software and hardware, connect with like-minded peers, and contribute to real-world projects.

    Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your journey, the Open Source Club is your gateway to making an impact in the world of open-source. Join us and be part of a community that believes in the power of open collaboration to shape the future of technology.
