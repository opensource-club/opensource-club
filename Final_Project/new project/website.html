The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, in collaboration with the Open Source Club, is conducting a 5-day bootcamp on web development fundamentals, covering HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. This intensive hands-on workshop aims to provide participants with practical skills in creating responsive and visually appealing websites using modern front-end technologies. This bootcamp is open to students of all skill levels, from beginners to those looking to refresh their web development knowledge. Participants will receive a certificate of completion at the end of the program.

Vision & Mission

At Open Source Club, in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), our mission is to build a vibrant community that empowers students to explore and excel in open-source technology. We champion collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning, aiming to be a leading platform for students to connect and grow.

Our vision is to inspire and support members through workshops, events, and networking opportunities. We strive to create a space where students of all levels can contribute meaningfully, advance their skills, and make an impact both within our college and in the broader tech community.

Join us to explore, learn, and grow together in a supportive and dynamic environment.
